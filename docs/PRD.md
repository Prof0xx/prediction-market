1. What this is:
   This is a decentralized prediction market platform on the Base blockchain, designed for short-duration events such as crypto price movements, esports outcomes, and meme coin narratives. It aims to provide users with fast, secure, and transparent betting experiences.

2. Who it's for:
   - Crypto Enthusiast: Interested in quick, engaging crypto market interactions.
   - Esports Fan: Wants to bet on esports match outcomes.
   - Meme Coin Trader: Interested in betting on meme coin trends and narratives.

3. Core features:
   1. Short-Duration Event Markets (P0)
   2. Decentralized Ledger for Transparency (P0)
   3. Instant Payouts (P0)
   4. User-Friendly Interface (P1)
   5. Market Creation Tools (P1)
   6. Social Sharing Features (P2)

4. What we are NOT building in v1:
   - Long-duration event markets
   - Complex financial derivatives or options trading
   - Native mobile applications
   - Advanced analytics or prediction algorithms

5. Success metrics:
   - 100 active users placing bets on Day 1
   - 1,000 active users with 500 daily transactions by Month 1
   - 10,000 active users and positive cash flow by Month 6
